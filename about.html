Timothy Goodman grew up in the vibrant neighborhood of New York City, where his childhood was filled with creativity and artistic influences. From an early age, he was captivated by the world around him, often expressing himself through drawing and painting. Encouraged by his family and the diverse cultural landscape of the city, he developed a keen interest in visual storytelling, which would later become a hallmark of his work.

Through dedication and a distinct voice, he carved out a successful career, making meaningful connections with audiences around the world.
