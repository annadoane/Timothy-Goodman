A notable highlight of his career includes creating the iconic cover art for Time magazine, where his vibrant, thought-provoking style conveyed deep cultural and social commentary. Goodman’s creativity also extends to the world of sports, where his artwork has been featured on Kevin Durant's signature shoes. His designs capture both the energy of basketball and the power of storytelling, creating a visual language that speaks to fans and athletes alike. His memoir, Sharpie Stories, provides a window into his personal journey, offering readers an intimate look at his evolution as an artist, his creative process, and the challenges he’s faced along the way. As a muralist, Goodman’s large-scale works continue to transform urban spaces, turning everyday walls into powerful, colorful statements that reflect both personal and collective experiences. Whether on the basketball court, the pages of Time, or on a mural in a bustling city, Timothy Goodman's work continues to push the boundaries of what art can be—connecting deeply with audiences through its bold, dynamic, and deeply human storytelling.
